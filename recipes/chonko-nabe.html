Chonko Nabe

Description

Chonko Nabe, also known as the sumo stew, is a type of nabemono, or "one-pot dish".

This dish is made up of a dashi base with sake and mirin. It is not a strict recipe and allows for you to empty out your fridge and throw whatever you want into the mix! This recipe will get you started, but feel free to change it up!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Stock
  • Sake
  • Mirin
  • Ginger juice
  • Crushed garlic
  • White miso

For the stew

  • Chicken meatballs
  • Cod fillet
  • Shirmp
  • Slice pork belly
  • Boneless, skinless chicken thigs
  • Medium-firm tofu
  • Napa cabbage
  • Tokyo negi
  • Green onion
  • Shiitake mushrooms
  • Carrot

For Serving

  • Ponzu
  • Sesame Dipping Sauce
  • Japanese short-grain rice
